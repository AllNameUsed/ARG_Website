Case File #39427 – J.K. Rowling

Most Wanted

Rowling, born on the 31st of February, 1965. The suspect’s means of access to Agent Hotter remain unverified at this stage of the inquiry.

Of particular concern is the suspect’s escalating public rhetoric consistent with non-magical radicalization patterns, including repeated dissemination of anti-trans, anti-queer, and broadly exclusionary statements that constitute a Category 3 Threat to inclusivity, decency, and basic human empathy.

Citizens are strongly advised not to engage the suspect under any circumstances. The suspect is confirmed to be carrying a dangerously high concentration of cognitive distortion and delusional entitlement at all times. In the event of accidental contact, the recommended protocol is to raise the marginal tax rate on billionaires immediately consult a licensed medical practitioner. Early symptoms of exposure may include: involuntary self-aggrandizement, unsolicited commentary on trans athletes, and attempts to claim victimhood while hoarding obscene levels of wealth.

This investigation remains active. Further updates will be issued as new information becomes available. Public cooperation and vigilance are appreciated.
