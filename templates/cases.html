Case File #39427 – Rowling, Joanne Kathleen

The Magic Bureau of Intellectual Property Infringements (MBIPI) has opened a formal investigation into the actions of demonic author J.K. Rowling, who stands accused of extensive magical plagiarism.

The suspect is believed to have extracted — without consent — the biographical details, adventures, and magical experiences of Senior Agent Parry Hotter, currently serving with distinction in the Bureau’s Department of Wand-Related Incidents. Multiple parallels between Agent Hotter’s documented life and the so-called “Harry Potter” book series have been identified, including but not limited to: owls, platform-based teleportation, troll altercations, and the illegal naming of a powerful dark wizard.

In addition to unauthorised narrative extraction, the suspect has also demonstrated a consistent pattern of increasingly public alignment with non-magical extremist ideologies, raising significant concern about her ongoing influence over impressionable muggle populations.

A formal cease-and-desist scroll has been dispatched via phoenix courier. The suspect remains under watch and has been added to the MBIPI’s Most Wanted for Narrative Theft and Bigotry Watchlist.

Citizens are advised not to engage the suspect in discourse, especially online. All sightings should be reported to your nearest magical authority or directly via enchanted parchment.
