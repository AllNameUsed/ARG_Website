About the Bureau

Our founder, the Grand Wizard Sir Isaac Newton, was born on 4 January 1643 under an especially mysterious star. He was a renowned alchemical theorist, transdimensional apple observer, and the first mortal to successfully command the sacred force of “gRaVItY”. Newton's work in light magic — splitting sunbeams with enchanted prisms — laid the foundation for modern illumination rituals and celestial ray-bending. His treatises on motion remain mandatory spellbooks in most licensed broomstick academies.

In his later years, Newton delved deeper into the esoteric arts, pursuing the ancient discipline of alchemistry and discovering the sacred correspondences between letters, numbers, and the raw chaos of the cosmos. It was through this mystical research that he pioneered the now-common practice of sigil crafting — using Pythagorean numerology to transmute ordinary names into powerful glyphs that whispered secrets to the divine. Some say his chalk circles are still spinning in parallel dimensions.
